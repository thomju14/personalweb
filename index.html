About me

I'm Thomas and And I have skills in

When I was 7 years old, I had my first contact with a computer - a 4.86 with Windows 3.11. I remember learning very quickly just by watching my uncles and grandmother using it. A few days later, I was using the computer without help. Time passed, and my family had a hard time because my father lost his job, so I had to start working early to help them. My passion for computers faded during this time because I didn't have the opportunity to develop it.

When I was 18 years old, I earned a scholarship in Managerial Production. That wasn't what I wanted, but it was the only way to get a better job to help my family and save money to find what I truly love to do. Many years later, I worked as a buyer for various companies and helped the IT department bring solutions. The passion for technology grew again, and I remembered my first contact and how I felt. Now, 24 years later, I have the opportunity to study and develop my skills.
